Pixabay and other external websites block other websites from playing their music directly without permission (this is what caused your "403 Forbidden" error!).

To get your music working:
1. Find any MP3 file you want to play for her (e.g., her favorite song).
2. Rename that file to exactly: `birthday.mp3`
3. Drag and drop that `birthday.mp3` file directly into this `public` folder alongside this text file!

When you reload your website, the music player will automatically find the file and start playing it.
